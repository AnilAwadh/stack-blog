Remember Stack Overflow Valued Associate #00002? Me neither. Until I saw these photographs of his developer lair, that is:

Never let it be said that I don't practice what I preach -- or rather, that I don't encourage my teammates to build themselves a proper developer lair!

Seen here:

  • Two 30" monitors and One 21" monitor for entry into the prestigious triple monitor club
  • A Herman Miller Mirra -- someone invested in a quality programming chair
  • Microsoft Natural 4000 -- the keyboard of the gods (well, in some religions)
  • Logitech G9 -- an excellent mouse
  • custom adjustable height keyboard podium
  • high end custom built developer rig (8GB, quad core, velociraptor, two video cards, etc)

And of course a fast internet connection. All part of the programmer's Bill of Rights!
