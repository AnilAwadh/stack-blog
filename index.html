About Us

Welcome! We are Stack Exchange, home of Stack Overflow, Stack Overflow Careers, and over 100 Q&A sites part of the Stack Exchange network. In addition to writing a lot of code, we like to write about our coding experiences and other things we’re doing, both at the company and in the tech space as a whole.

If you’d like to keep up with company initiatives and other happenings at Stack Exchange, check out the Company News channel. If you want to learn about some of our coding projects, mishaps, and other tech details, explore the Engineering channel.

We hope you enjoy reading it as much as we enjoy sharing it with you!
